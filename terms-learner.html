Terms and Conditions for Students & Guardians

Effective Date: June 2026 | Version 1.0

Welcome to Skolar. By registering an account as a user or a supervising parent/guardian, you accept and agree to comply with these Terms and Conditions. These policies guarantee a fair, reliable, and mutually respectful atmosphere for both students and educators.

1. Eligibility and Guardian Responsibility

Safety is Skolar's highest operational priority. All platform participants must verify their identity.

  • Mandatory Verification: Students are required to provide a valid, unexpired government-issued photo identity document upon registration.
  • Minor Policy (Under 18): If the student is a minor, a parent or legal guardian must create and manage the account. The guardian must upload their own valid government photo ID and is legally responsible for monitoring all platform activities, communications, and scheduling.
  • Data Security: All identification documentation is fully encrypted, securely stored, and reviewed strictly by Skolar Administration. It will never be exposed to tutors or other third parties.

2. Session Structure Requirements

  • Minimum Session Duration: To maximize learning efficacy and respect educators' schedules, all bookings require a minimum length of two (2) hours per session. Shorter time frames or partial checkouts are not supported by the platform.

3.1 Advanced Cancellation Warning System

While providing more than 3 days' notice grants a full financial refund, excessive cancellations disrupt tutor schedules and platform resource allocation.

  • Each 3+ day cancellation registers an official system warning.
  • Every third (3rd) warning an account accumulates will automatically reduce the student's next 3+ day cancellation refund from 100% down to 50% only. Subsequent warnings reset the tracking block.

💡 Tutor Cancellation Protections for Students

If a tutor cancels on you last-minute or on the same day, you are fully protected. You receive a 100% refund immediately, plus Skolar will award you a 20% discount voucher to apply to your next 2-hour session booking.

⚠ Anti-Circumvention Rule

To protect your financial security and privacy, all scheduling, communications, and financial exchange must take place strictly inside Skolar. Engaging in off-platform cash or app payments, or exchanging direct contact details to bypass platform protections, constitutes a major violation and results in the immediate and irreversible closure of your account.

4. Exclusive Administrative Review

Any and all complaints, disputes, tutor misconduct reports, or fee discrepancies must be submitted exclusively to the Skolar Administration.

  • Students and guardians agree that administrative intervention is the designated path for dispute resolution.
  • All decisions rendered by Skolar Administration regarding cancellations, partial refunds, warning distribution, or user suspensions are final, absolute, and binding.

5. Academic Integrity Code

Skolar is an educational growth tool designed to foster self-sufficiency. Students are expected to uphold the rules of their primary academic institutions.

  • Students may not demand, request, or coerce tutors into completing graded homework, writing papers, or taking tests on their behalf.
  • Using Skolar services to commit academic fraud or cheating will cause immediate administrative review and potential account suspension.

6. Liability Disclaimer

Skolar serves as an intermediary platform connecting users. We ensure identity validation and secure environments but are not liable for specific test scores, classroom grades, or individual tutor methods. Users must treat all educators with respect during live video interfaces.
