How TutorHub works

For Learners

1

Visit the learner page and view the tutor’s information.

2

Select an available date from the tutor’s calendar.

3

Complete the booking form and wait for confirmation.

For Tutors

1

Log in to the tutor dashboard.

2

Add availability and manage incoming bookings.

3

Approve reviews before they appear publicly.

✓
